Riemann Zeta Zeros Validation: Methodology and Results
============================================================

METHODOLOGY:
------------
1. High-precision computation of 1000 Riemann zeta zeros
   - Used mpmath library with 50 decimal precision
   - Computed imaginary parts of non-trivial zeros

2. Unfolding transformation applied:
   - Formula: tilde_t = t / (2π log(t / (2π e)))
   - Purpose: Normalize average spacing to unity for universal analysis

3. Spacing statistics analysis:
   - Computed nearest neighbor spacings
   - Normalized to unit mean
   - Compared to GUE (Gaussian Unitary Ensemble) predictions
