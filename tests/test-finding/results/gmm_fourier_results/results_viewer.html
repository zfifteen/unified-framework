Analysis Summary

Objective: Fit GMM to θ' distributions and compute Fourier asymmetry at k=0.3

Data: 78,498 primes up to 10⁶, transformed using θ'(p,k=0.3) = φ·((p mod φ)/φ)^k

Methods: 5-component GMM with StandardScaler, 5-harmonic Fourier series, 1000-iteration bootstrap

Key Finding: Strong Fourier asymmetry (S_b = 2.325) and tight GMM clustering (bar_σ = 0.062) demonstrate non-uniform prime distributions under golden ratio curvature transformation
